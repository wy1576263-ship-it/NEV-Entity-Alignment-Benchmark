# NEV Knowledge Graph & Entity Alignment Benchmark

This repository contains the dataset, knowledge graph construction scripts, and the evaluation benchmark for the **Weighted Fusion Entity Alignment** strategy described in our paper. 

Our research focuses on resolving entity redundancy caused by domain-specific synonyms and colloquial abbreviations in the New Energy Vehicle (NEV) domain.

> **💡 System Interface Demonstration:**
> Beyond the algorithmic evaluation, we have developed a complete Neo4j-based full-stack QA prototype for the NEV domain. **Please refer to the Figures in our manuscript for the detailed visual demonstrations of the system interface** (including the data dashboard and D3.js knowledge graph visualization). The frontend HTML/JS source code is tightly coupled with our internal proprietary database and is omitted here to maintain algorithmic focus.

## 🔐 Data Availability & NDA Compliance Statement

This research is conducted using real-world diagnostic records from our industry partners. To strictly comply with the **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** and data privacy regulations, the following restrictions apply to this open-source repository:

1. **Proprietary Models & Raw Corpus**: The full 150,000 raw QA diagnostic corpus and the fine-tuned proprietary BERT model weights are highly confidential corporate assets and **cannot be distributed**.
2. **Knowledge Graph Topology**: We release the core topological data (`New_Ontology_Edges.csv` with ~14k edges) and raw vehicle parameters (`Vehicle_Parameters.csv`) to demonstrate the industrial scale and architectural validity of the NEV Knowledge Graph.
3. **Desensitized Academic Benchmark**: To ensure scientific reproducibility of our alignment algorithm without compromising sensitive data, we provide a rigorously desensitized subset of 200 confusing entity pairs (`confusing_entities_200.csv`) along with their corresponding semantic inference logits (`bert_output_logits.csv`) generated by our private model.

## 📂 Repository Structure & Pipeline

* **`Data/`**: Contains the KG topology data, vehicle parameters, and the 200-pair ablation study benchmark.
* **`Scripts/`**: 
  * `kg_build_ontology.py`: Initializes the base four-dimensional ontology in Neo4j.
  * `kg_merge_parameters.py`: Performs Knowledge Fusion by mounting heterogenous vehicle parameters onto the base ontology.
  * `reproduce_table_1.py`: The evaluation script to verify the alignment algorithm.
